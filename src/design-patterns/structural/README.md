# Structural Patterns

Structural patterns deal with how classes and objects are composed to form larger structures, while keeping them flexible and efficient.

## The Patterns

| Pattern | Mnemonic |
|---------|----------|
| **Adapter** | A power plug adapter that lets a US plug fit a European socket |
| **Bridge** | A TV remote control that works with any device independently |
| **Composite** | A military hierarchy where orders cascade down the chain |
| **Decorator** | Wearing layers of clothes — each adds behavior without changing you |
| **Facade** | A phone shop operator that handles everything behind the scenes |
| **Flyweight** | A forest where millions of trees share the same texture data |
| **Proxy** | A credit card that controls access to your bank account |
